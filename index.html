Hemorrhoid Creams: Information and Uses.

What does hemorrhoid cream do?

Hemorrhoid cream is a topical treatment applied to the exterior or interior of the anal area. It aims to alleviate symptoms associated with hemorrhoids, such as swelling, pain, and itching. These creams often contain active ingredients like hydrocortisone or witch hazel, which help reduce inflammation and provide soothing relief.
